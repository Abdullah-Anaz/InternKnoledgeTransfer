Who is ElziAN Agro

AGRO provides smart agronomy solutions to support mass cultivation farmers to monitor and automate their farmland. According to United Nations Sustainable Reports, farmers negatively impact due to extreme weather changes and limited resources. ELZIAN AGRO is a start-up that provides a soil and weather monitoring system for indoor and mass cultivation farmers and supports them to automate their farmland. We offer customized monitoring information and automation solutions through the sensor network to optimize our customers' harvest and productively utilize their resources. Our customers can monitor their farmland anywhere, anytime, and make efficient and rational decisions to maximize their profitability.
